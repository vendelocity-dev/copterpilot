What we'll Do

First we'll prep at our hanger, where you'll learn tricks mastered during career providing aerial footage for movies, TV, and music videos. Then we'll soar above the city with the doors off as we teach you how to get movie-perfect shots. Back on the ground, we'll cap off an exhilarating afternoon with a group photo with your pilot and crew in front of the helicopter.
